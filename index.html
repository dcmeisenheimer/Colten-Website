Colten Jenison
Fashion Designer from Enterprise, AL
Tuscaloosa, Alabama

My name is Colten Jenison and I am from Enterprise, Alabama. I am an Apparel and Textiles major at the University of Alabama and graduate with a BS in May 2022. As a designer I am inspired by my hometown and the south along with music and movies. During my time at Alabama I have learned a lot about my creative view and the direction I want to go with it. The resources available to there have helped me create projects such as my senior thesis collection which was Rolling Stones inspired. I am always looking to learn new things with collections and open to all viewpoints when it comes to design.
